3: Definition
Poverty is not earning enough money to support your family. The "poverty line" is the line where if you make less money, then you are considered to be in poverty.
4: Illustration
This is an illustration of the struggles of freeing oneself from poverty. The person wants to get a better life, but is held back by poverty.
5: 3 causes
  1. Overpopulation means that there is more people that together get the same amount of money.
  2. Lack of education means that less people get better-paying jobs.
  3. Being born into poverty means you will most likely grow up impoverished.
6: 3 effects
  1. Having more people in poverty lowers other countries' opinions of that country.
  2. More impoverished people increases disease rates.
  3. Poverty is a very difficult problem to overcome.
