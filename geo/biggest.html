I feel that the biggest problem that India faces is overpopulation. I feel this way for 5 reasons:

  1. Poverty stems from overpopulation.
  2. If the population continues to rise, India will have to import food and other commodities until it is in debt.
  3. India will also have to export waste.
  4. Poverty can be solved easily with outside help, but overpopulation requires emigration or death.
  5. Kashmir can be a rallying point for the people to bring them out of poverty, similar to WWII for the US.
