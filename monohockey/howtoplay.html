• MonoHockey is a single-player air hockey variant.
  • You control the green circle, the 'pusher', with your mouse.
  • The objective is to get the red puck into the goal at the top.
  • Your pusher can't hit the red puck.
  • You must hit the grey puck into the red puck to move the red puck.
  • Be careful! If the grey puck goes out through the goal, there is no way to hit the red puck and you will lose.
