KeySwap is a typing game, of sorts.

  • To start a game, simply press any button on your keyboard.
  • The text under the KeySwap will begin to scroll to the left.
  • Start typing the word 'begin'.
  • As you type, the letters will change to green.
  • If any red letters make it off the left side of the screen, you lose!
  • More random words will appear from the right.
  • Occasionally, bewtween the row of words and the KeySwap title, you will see a notification that looks like:

    • A is switched with B

  • While this is displayed, these two characters are switched.
  • If the screen said bad, you would actually type abd.
  • Pay attention! It is not always A and B that are switched.
